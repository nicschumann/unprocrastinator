FAQ

How do I create a task?

To create a new task, begin writing the task's category in a new field. Our system will auto-complete the category name, just press enter on the correct category! Once a category is selected, continue typing in the task name. Press enter to create your new task. If you wish to add more information to your task, like subtasks or tags, just click on the task name to view and edit task details.

What is a category?

A category helps you define the type of tasks. As students, we use categories like "CS132" to designate assignments based on class. Every category comes with a color, so you can know which class an assignment is for at a glance. If you wish to create a new category, you can do so by typing the category name into a new task field and saving it as a new category. It will then be saved so you can use it again and utilize Unprocrastinator's time-managing aid!

What does the progress bar mean?

Unprocrastinator is specifically designed for students like us - procrastinators who perpetually underestimate how long assignments take! That's why we added a time-management component to this application so that students could get a real idea of how long tasks take them. To use the progress bar, add tags to your task and update your hours logged either manually or with our built in timer. When you check off an assignment as complete, Unprocrastinator saves your time and uses it to approximate how long tasks with those same tags will take, and shows you how far along you are with the progress bar.

What is the difference between a due date and an assigned date?

The due date is exactly what it sounds like - the day the task is due. The assigned date is what day you plan to work on a task. This lets you keep track of when you need to be finished but also keeps your to-do list from getting cluttered with tasks you don't plan to do.

How do I reschedule a task?

To reschedule a task, you can change the assigned date by opening the task details and clicking on the calendar icon. From there, you can edit either the assigned date to change what day you plan to work on the task or the due date (which is automatically set to the assigned date when a task is first created).

What happens to old tasks?

Old tasks that have been completed will not show up in your calendar, but their time data will be stored to improve time-management for tasks with the same tags. If you have an incomplete task with a past assigned date, the task will display on today's to-do list with an option to reschedule when you want to work on it.

Do I need to add my estimated time to completion?

If you wish to use the time-management functionality of Unprocrastinator, you must add an estimated time to completion for new types of tasks and new categories. This is to help Unprocrastinator get a general sense of how long an assignment should take you, and this time will be updated as you complete more tasks of the same nature. You might be surprised how easily you underestimate your tasks!
